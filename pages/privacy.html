Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 08/04/2026

1. Introduction

Your trust matters. QMath (developed by Kyra Apps) is designed to be simple, safe, and transparent. We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or location. This policy explains how limited non-personal data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, or phone number.

Automatic Data Collection

QMath uses third-party services such as Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob (a service by Google) which may automatically collect limited non-personal data such as device information, app usage (e.g., screen views, session duration), and basic diagnostic data. This information is collected in an anonymous and aggregated form.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To understand how users interact with the app
  • To fix bugs and enhance features

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

QMath uses trusted third-party services to improve app functionality:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) – for app usage analysis
  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements

These services may process limited non-personal data as described in their own privacy policies.

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not store user-specific information on our servers. Anonymous analytics data may be retained by Firebase as per Google's policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. Firebase services use secure (encrypted) transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

QMath is intended for general audiences (13+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe any data has been collected improperly, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app means you accept the changes.

9. Local Storage (on your device)

All game data such as coins, badges, and progress is stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on our servers.

10. Third-Party Assets

QMath uses open-source and third-party resources such as:

  • Bootstrap Icons (open-source icon library)
  • Google Fonts (web fonts service by Google)
  • Some Icons from Flaticon (by Freepik)
  • Howler.js – JavaScript audio library used for sound effects

These services may have their own privacy policies and terms.

11. Important Disclosure

QMath includes a coin-based reward system:

  • Coins and badges have no real-world monetary value
  • They cannot be exchanged for cash or real rewards
  • They are used only for gameplay and achievements
